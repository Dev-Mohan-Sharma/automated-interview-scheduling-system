# Automated Interview Scheduling System (AISS)

## Overview
This project addresses inefficiencies in manual interview scheduling such as delays, rescheduling conflicts, and high recruiter effort.

## Objective
To automate interview scheduling by integrating interviewer availability, buffer rules, and ATS updates.
